I am Krrish Ghindani

Hello, I'm Krrish Ghindani, a recent graduate from The Emerald Heights International School in Indore, India, where my passion for academics and technology thrives. As a researcher, I take pride in having published three research papers in esteemed international journals. This experience has honed my skills in conducting thorough research, analyzing data, and effectively presenting findings that contribute to the advancement of knowledge in my field.

I am the founder of Forbes Student Research, and initiative to equip high school students with limited opportunities with the tools and resources needed to pursue their academic and career goals. We offer free-of-cost assistance with writing research papers, proofreading services, and opportunities for publication in international journals. Our vision is to enhance the academic culture by providing educational opportunities that ignite curiosity, inspire creativity, and make a lasting impact on students' lives. Through this initiative, we aim to foster international peer collaboration and challenge assumptions while breaking down stereotypes in the academic realm.

In my role as a Jr. Front-End Developer, I have delved deep into the world of technology, refining my analytical and problem-solving abilities. Proficient in programming languages like Python, HTML5, CSS, and JavaScript, I enjoy the challenge of learning new technologies and using them to create innovative solutions.
